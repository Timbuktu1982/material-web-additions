## Example Usage
### Standard

<Canvas
    mdxSource={dedent`
        <md-elevated-card>
          Content
        </md-elevated-card>
    `}>
    <md-elevated-card outlined style={{'--md-card-padding': '16px'}}>
        Content
    </md-elevated-card>
</Canvas>

<blockquote>
    You may want to apply padding to card contents (recommended to `16px` by the Material Design guidelines).
    In the previous live example and the following one the padding is already applied.
    ```css
    {`
md-elevated-card {
    --md-card-padding: 16px;
}`}
    ```
</blockquote>


### Outlined card

To use an outlined card, use the `md-outlined-card` element.

<Canvas
    mdxSource={dedent`
        <md-outlined-card>
          Content
        </md-outlined-card>
    `}>
    <md-outlined-card outlined style={{'--md-card-padding': '16px'}}>
        Content
    </md-outlined-card>
</Canvas>

### Primary action
If most of the card should be actionable, you can add the `clickable` attribute to your card element.

<Canvas
    mdxSource={dedent`
        <md-elevated-card clickable class="demo-card">
            <div class="demo-card__header">
                <div class="demo-card__header-text">
                    <div class="demo-card__title">Our Changing Planet</div>
                    <div class="demo-card__subtitle">by Kurt Wagner</div>
                </div>
            </div>
        </md-elevated-card>
    `}>
    <md-elevated-card clickable class="demo-card">
        <div className="demo-card__header">
            <div className="demo-card__header-text">
                <div className="demo-card__title">Our Changing Planet</div>
                <div className="demo-card__subtitle">by Kurt Wagner</div>
            </div>
        </div>
    </md-elevated-card>
</Canvas>

### Media
If you want to display an image in the card, you can add an image with a standard `<img>` element.

<Canvas
    mdxSource={dedent`
        <md-elevated-card class="demo-card">
            <img src="https://cdn.glitch.me/9c389208-b279-4e96-bcbc-e5f8712d8706%2Fplaceholder-landscape-short.png" alt="Background photo" class="demo-card__media" />
            <div class="demo-card__header">
                <div class="demo-card__header-text">
                    <div class="demo-card__title">Our Changing Planet</div>
                    <div class="demo-card__subtitle">by Kurt Wagner</div>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="demo-card__secondary mdc-typography mdc-typography--body2">
                Visit ten places on our planet that are undergoing the biggest changes today.
            </div>
        </md-elevated-card>
    `}>
    <md-elevated-card class="demo-card">
        <img src="https://cdn.glitch.me/9c389208-b279-4e96-bcbc-e5f8712d8706%2Fplaceholder-landscape-short.png"
             alt="Background photo" className="demo-card__media"/>
        <div className="demo-card__header">
            <div className="demo-card__header-text">
                <div className="demo-card__title">Our Changing Planet</div>
                <div className="demo-card__subtitle">by Kurt Wagner</div>
            </div>
        </div>
        <div className="demo-card__secondary mdc-typography mdc-typography--body2">
            Visit ten places on our planet that are undergoing the biggest changes today.
        </div>
    </md-elevated-card>
</Canvas>

### Actions
